# Cruzeiro Esporte Clube Website

## Project Overview

This is a professional, fully responsive website dedicated to **Cruzeiro Esporte Clube**, one of the most successful football clubs in South American history. The website is built entirely in English and showcases the club's rich history, achievements, players, and passion.

## Website Structure

The website consists of five main pages:

### 1. **Home (index.html)**
The landing page featuring:
- A welcoming introduction to Cruzeiro
- The club's tagline: "The Pride of Belo Horizonte"
- Key highlights about the club's achievements
- Call-to-action buttons to explore more content

### 2. **About (about.html)**
Comprehensive information about the club including:
- The club's founding history (January 2, 1921)
- The name change from Palestra Itália to Cruzeiro in 1942
- Club identity: nickname "A Raposa" (The Fox), colors (Blue and White)
- Information about Mineirão Stadium
- The golden era of the 1970s and 1990s
- Legacy and impact on world football

### 3. **Titles (titles.html)**
A detailed showcase of the club's achievements:
- Copa Libertadores: 2 titles (1976, 1997)
- Campeonato Brasileiro: 4 titles (1966, 2003, 2013, 2014)
- Copa do Brasil: 6 titles (Record holder) (1993, 1996, 2000, 2003, 2017, 2018)
- Campeonato Mineiro: 38 titles
- Other notable achievements

### 4. **Players (players.html)**
Profiles of key squad members:
- **Cássio** (Goalkeeper)
- **Marlon** (Defender)
- **Lucas Silva** (Midfielder)
- **Matheus Pereira** (Midfielder)
- **Kaio Jorge** (Forward)
- **Henrique** (Defender)

Each player profile includes their position and a descriptive summary of their skills and role in the team.

### 5. **Opinion (opinion.html)**
A personal perspective on why Cruzeiro is special, covering:
- The club's beautiful history and resilience
- Record-breaking achievements in Copa do Brasil
- The unique atmosphere at Mineirão Stadium
- The tradition of developing world-class talent
- The spirit of "A Raposa" and its tactical brilliance
- Cruzeiro's significance to Belo Horizonte and Minas Gerais

## About Cruzeiro Esporte Clube

**Cruzeiro Esporte Clube** is a Brazilian football club based in Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais. Founded on January 2, 1921, as Palestra Itália, the club changed its name to Cruzeiro in 1942. Known as "A Raposa" (The Fox), Cruzeiro is one of the most successful clubs in South American football history.

### Key Facts
- **Founded:** January 2, 1921
- **Stadium:** Mineirão (Estádio Governador Magalhães Pinto)
- **City:** Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais, Brazil
- **Nickname:** A Raposa (The Fox), Celeste (The Celestial)
- **Colors:** Blue and White
- **Record:** 6 Copa do Brasil titles (most in Brazilian football)
